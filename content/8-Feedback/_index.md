While participating in the First Cloud Journey (FCAJ) program, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and experience a real working environment through learning activities, workshops, meetings, and group project development. This program provided immense value, helping me improve my technical knowledge as well as the essential skills for career development.

Below are some of my personal thoughts and feedback after completing the program:
## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Learning and Working Environment

One of the aspects I appreciated most about **First Cloud Journey (FCAJ) program** was its friendly and supportive learning environment. Although the program was conducted mainly online, the administrators were always willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties. Everyone was open to sharing their knowledge and experience, making it comfortable to ask questions and learn from one another.

### 2. Support from the Program Administrators

The administrators provided excellent support throughout the internship. Whenever I faced challenges while practicing AWS services or developing the project, they guided me through the problem-solving process instead of simply providing the answers. This approach helped me better understand the concepts and improve my ability to troubleshoot issues independently.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Major

The internship program was highly relevant to my major in Information Technology. It not only strengthened my understanding of cloud computing but also introduced me to many AWS services widely used in real-world environments, such as Amazon EC2, Amazon VPC, Amazon S3, Amazon RDS, AWS IAM, AWS Lambda, Amazon CloudWatch, and others. Applying these services to a real project gave me valuable practical experience.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities

Throughout the internship, I gained both technical knowledge and practical experience. Besides learning AWS technologies, I also improved my understanding of software development workflows, cloud architecture design, system optimization, testing, and project documentation. These experiences complemented what I had learned at university and helped prepare me for a professional working environment.

### 5. Team Culture and Collaboration

The collaborative spirit within the program was one of its greatest strengths. The administrators and participants were always willing to share resources, discuss ideas, and support one another. This positive atmosphere motivated me to continue learning and successfully complete the internship.

### 6. Internship Program

I appreciate that First Cloud Journey (FCAJ) program provides a well-structured learning roadmap, progressing from fundamental concepts to more advanced AWS services. The workshops effectively combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice, making it easier to understand and apply what was learned in real-world scenarios.

---

## Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying part of your internship?

The most rewarding aspect of the internship was the opportunity to follow a structured AWS learning path and apply the acquired knowledge directly to the **AuraAcademic** project. I also greatly appreciated the guidance and encouragement provided by the administrators throughout the program.

### What do you think could be improved?

I believe the program could include more advanced workshops focusing on real-world AWS deployment scenarios, such as cost optimization, cloud security, system monitoring, and production troubleshooting. These topics would help participants better understand enterprise-level cloud solutions.

### Would you recommend this program to your friends?

Yes, absolutely. I would highly recommend First Cloud Journey (FCAJ) program to anyone interested in Cloud Computing or Amazon Web Services. The program offers a clear learning roadmap, plenty of hands-on practice, and dedicated support from experienced administrators, making it suitable for both beginners and learners who want to deepen their AWS knowledge.

---

## Suggestions and Expectations

- Continue updating the workshop content to include the latest AWS services and best practices.
- Organize more experience-sharing sessions with cloud engineers and DevOps professionals working in the industry.
- Provide additional hands-on labs and real-world project scenarios to help participants gain practical experience.
- I hope the program continues to grow and provides more students with the opportunity to learn AWS and cloud computing technologies.

### Final Thoughts

Participating in First Cloud Journey (FCAJ) program has been a valuable and memorable experience. It not only strengthened my technical knowledge of AWS but also improved my self-learning ability, problem-solving skills, teamwork, and communication. I sincerely appreciate the dedication and support of the program administrators throughout the internship. This experience has provided a strong foundation for my future career in cloud computing and software development.
